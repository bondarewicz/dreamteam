They are raw and unfiltered: Product Manager: "Express deliveries are guaranteed within 2 hours. If we miss the window, we refund the express fee but not the base delivery fee. Legal: "We cannot guarantee a 2-hour delivery time in writing — this is a 'best effort' SLA, not a contractual guarantee. The refund is a goodwill gesture, not legally enforceable." Synthesize a domain model from these notes. Flag all contradictions and gaps.
