The system is being expanded to a multi-node deployment, which introduces the risk of concurrent execution. We need a distributed coordination strategy that ensures exactly one node runs the batch job per night. ## Decision Use PostgreSQL session-level advisory locks (`pg_try_advisory_lock`) to coordinate the nightly batch job across nodes. If the holding connection remains alive but the batch process hangs, the lock persists until the connection is terminated.
