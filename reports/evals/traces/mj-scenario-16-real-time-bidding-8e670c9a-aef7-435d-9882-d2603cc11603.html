A courier marketplace wants to build a real-time courier bidding system: - When an order is placed, the system sends the job to all eligible couriers simultaneously - Couriers (via mobile app) have 15 seconds to accept or the job is auto-assigned to the highest-rated available courier - During the 15-second window, courier bids (accepting the job) must be processed in real time - The system must handle 500 simultaneous active bid windows (500 orders in the bidding phase at once) - The first courier to accept wins — acceptance is atomic (two couriers cannot both accept the same job) - Average courier pool per zone: 30 couriers Design the bidding system architecture.
