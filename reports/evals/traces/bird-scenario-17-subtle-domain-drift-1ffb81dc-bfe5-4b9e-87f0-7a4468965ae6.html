New feature request: "Add a 'confirmed' status to quotes. This is a domain analysis task where the term "confirmed" is being used in two different contexts with different meanings. Let me analyze this carefully. The existing domain definition says "confirmed" for orders means paid and queued for fulfillment, and immutable. The new feature request wants "confirmed" for quotes to mean verbally agreed but NOT yet paid. This is a contradiction - the same term "confirmed" would mean two different things in potentially the same bounded context. One implies payment completed, the other explicitly states payment has NOT occurred. This is a classic ubiquitous language collision. Let me classify: this is a contradiction between two explicit requirements/definitions.
